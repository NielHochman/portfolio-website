About Me

Hello! I'm Niel Hochman, a creative game developer and tech artist with a passion for bringing characters and worlds to life. I blend technical expertise with artistic vision to create engaging experiences that captivate audiences across different platforms.

With a background in animation (studied at Animation Mentor), game development, and technical art, I've worked on diverse projects from character animations to interactive game experiences. I love exploring the intersection of storytelling, visual design, and interactive media.

My approach combines technical precision with creative imagination, allowing me to transform concepts into polished, professional games and visual experiences that connect with audiences.
