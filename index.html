About Me

Hello! I'm Niel Hochman, a creative animator and game developer with a passion for bringing characters and worlds to life. I blend technical expertise with artistic vision to create engaging experiences that captivate audiences across different platforms.

My approach combines technical precision with creative imagination, allowing me to transform concepts into polished, professional animations and game elements that connect with audiences and enhance user experiences.
