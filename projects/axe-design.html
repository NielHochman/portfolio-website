Sci-Fi Robot

3D Character Design

About This Project

This sci-fi robot character was designed for a futuristic game environment. The design combines sleek, modern aesthetics with functional mechanical elements to create a believable robotic character that could exist in a high-tech world.

I focused on creating a balance between mechanical precision and character personality, incorporating design elements that suggest intelligence and purpose. The materials and lighting were carefully crafted to highlight the metallic surfaces and glowing energy components that power the robot.
