Stylized Home

3D Environment Design

About This Project

This stylized home environment showcases my approach to creating visually engaging 3D spaces with a unique artistic flair. The design blends modern architectural elements with stylized proportions and vibrant colors to create a space that feels both inviting and imaginative.

I focused on creating a balanced composition with careful attention to lighting, materials, and spatial arrangement. The stylized approach allowed me to emphasize certain architectural features while maintaining a cohesive and appealing overall aesthetic that could work well in animated content or stylized game environments.
